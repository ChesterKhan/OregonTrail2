Strategies

Map

Oregon Trail II is an expansive game with many choices. Veterans of the Oregon Trail already know what to do: buy oxen, supplies you might need, try to keep your party from dying, wipe out buffalo by hunting, and hopefully you'll make it to Oregon in one piece.

But do you want the high score?

Here's what I do to get it:

  1. At the very beginning, have as many people as you can: five. Keeping as many people as possible in good health is the best thing you can do for your score.
  2. Choose the "farmer" profession. Farmers get some useful skills and a big score multiplier at the end of the game.
  3. Under the "skills" tab, choose the following skills: medical, commerce, carpentry or blacksmithing, and musical.
    • Medical skills will make survival easier.
    • Commerce will make getting money later easier.
    • Carpentry will make it easier to repair your wagon and save wagon parts.
    • Musical will give you a regular morale boost.
